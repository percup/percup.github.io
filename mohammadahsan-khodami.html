About Me?

My former research were including Emotion Regulation, the impact of Covid-19 on health, Stress especially perceived stress and its impact on attention and inhibition.

Since Nov 2023 when I joined the University of Padua, My main research areas are including vision, visual processing speed, hemianopia, attention, and cognition. I am also partially interested in doing research on memory including Working Memory (WM) and the impact of attention on it.

I am interested in using imaging techniques such as EEG and also Eye-tracking and following the results of psychophysical training on Visual Processing Speed (VPS), in patients with Hemianopia. Further, using techniques of Machine Learning (ML), I use ML to analyze data.
